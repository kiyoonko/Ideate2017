Who We Are

Technology

As a group of individuals dedicated to technology, we inspire everyone to explore all potentials and possibilities in tech.

Mentorship

We help each other navigate our career paths by sharing experiences and advice on selecting classes, finding opportunities, preparing for interviews, and more.

Community

We're a diverse, tight-knit group of people who can rely on each other for not only career and academic advice, but also for social and emotional support.
